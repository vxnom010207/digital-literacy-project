# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone  
- Always verify payment requests before approving  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Use only trusted and official apps for transactions  
- Enable two-factor authentication on your accounts  
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money  
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly  

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Remember that you do not need to enter a PIN to receive money  
- Always check the source before approving any payment request  

## Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
